## Abstract
To ensure future food security, the gap between actual yields and potential yields must be closed. This review defines the methodology for calculating Yield Gaps (Yg)...

## Key Methodologies Defined
1. **Potential Yield (Yp):** Calculated using crop simulation models (e.g., WOFOST, DSSAT) assuming non-limiting water and nutrients.
2. **Water-Limited Yield (Yw):** For rainfed systems, dependent on rainfall.
3. **Actual Yield (Ya):** Observed farm data.
